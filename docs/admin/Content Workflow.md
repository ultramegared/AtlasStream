# Content Workflow

## Overview

This document describes the lifecycle of content inside AtlasStream.

—

## Workflow

Draft

↓

Review

↓

Ready

↓

Published

↓

Archived

—

## Draft

Content is being created.

Only administrators can access it.

—

## Review

Content is reviewed before publication.

Images, videos and metadata are verified.

—

## Ready

Content is complete and ready to publish.

—

## Published

Content is available to users.

—

## Archived

Content is hidden from users but kept for history.

—

## Notes

Content can move between stages when necessary.

Publishing should never require recreating the content.
