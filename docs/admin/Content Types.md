# Content Types

## Overview

AtlasStream supports multiple types of multimedia content.

—

## Movie

Standalone video content.

—

## Series

Collection of seasons and episodes.

—

## Live TV

Live television channels.

—

## Documentary

Educational or informational productions.

—

## Cartoon

Animated content.

—

## Anime

Japanese animated series and movies.

—

## Sports

Live and recorded sporting events.

—

## Music

Concerts, music videos and live performances.

—

## Kids

Family-friendly entertainment.

—

## Future Content

The platform architecture allows adding new content types without modifying the existing structure.
